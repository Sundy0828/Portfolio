About Me

I am a year old Computer Programmer from Altoona, PA. Since the 7th grade, I've gained an abundence of programming experience. I first became interested in computers in 7th grade. I taught myself HTML and CSS for fun, and my first formal project was to create a website with a partner for a TSA (Technology Student Association) competition – ultimately placing third in the state. My team and I entered another website design competition later that year at Penn State Altoona placing second. I then attended, GACTC (Greater Altoona Career and Technology Center), a vocational school where I learned COBAL, RPG, SQL, and Visual Basic, I used this knowledge to build programs that were eventually used by the school. After I graduated, I began my undergraduate studies at Seton Hill University, with a double major in Computer Science and Cybersecurity in 2015. I am currently a member of the Resident Housing Association where I am able to help bring the comunity together. I am also a team captain of the cross country and track and field teams which allows me to lead by pacing workouts or finding new routes and to help everyone surpass their limits. I am also the event organizer for the computer science club, where I create and coordinate events such as building robots and in-house programming competitions. I have taken a number of classes for computer science in my 3 years here, and have learned many different skills in Languages, Applications, and Operating Systems.
